The Stack

In this assignment I will be outlining the different parts of The Stack as outlined by Benjamin Bratton through 6 different images. You can view the images below:

1: User

In the first assignment I talked about how technology has desocialized us. In particular it has stripped kids of the social skills that need to be fostered when they are young.

2: Interface

The Interface level shows the applications that children and adults are generally using. While these applications can have positive impacts they are also consistently used for scamming, cyber-bullying and time-wasting.

3: Address

The address level goes into how individuals are able to actively use all of the apps on their smartphones and gadgets. Generally this is achieved through their home wifi or through data covered by your cellphone plan.

4: City

The City level goes into all of the other people around you that are additionally on their phones around you on the same servers and data services that you are using.

5: Cloud

In the cloud layer all of the data from the moment in which you are on your phone is data compiled from data centers across the world.

6: Earth

In the Earth layer are all of the individuals that are on their phones instead of spending time with their families. The positive and negative impacts of technology go hand in hand. Although you are able to keep in touch with people thousands of miles away and are able to learn and have so much at your fingertips, it is difficult to prevent ourselves from spending too much time on our devices and taking away from the real interactions that make life worth living.
